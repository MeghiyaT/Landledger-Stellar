## ❓ Why Can't I Use PROP Tokens?

**Technical Explanation:**
- Gas fees are paid to Ethereum network validators
- Validators only accept ETH (native token)
- PROP tokens are ERC-20 tokens (smart contract tokens)
- ERC-20 tokens cannot be used for gas fees
- This is a fundamental blockchain limitation, not something we can change

**Analogy:**
- Think of ETH as "cash" for the network
- PROP tokens are like "gift cards" - useful for transactions, but can't pay for network fees
