## ❓ Troubleshooting

**ETH hasn't arrived?**
- Wait 5-10 minutes (some faucets are slow)
- Make sure you're on Sepolia network in MetaMask
- Check the faucet's status page for delays
- Try a different faucet

**Faucet says "already claimed"?**
- Most faucets have daily limits
- Wait 24 hours and try again
- Try a different faucet in the meantime

**Need more ETH?**
- Use multiple faucets on different days
- Some faucets allow multiple claims per day
- Consider getting a small amount of mainnet ETH (0.001) to access premium faucets
